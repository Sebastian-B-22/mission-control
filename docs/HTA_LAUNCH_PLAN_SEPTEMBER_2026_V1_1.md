# HTA Launch Plan - September 2026 (v1.1 FINAL)

July 7, 2026. Supersedes v1.0. All open decisions now made by
Corinne. Distribute to Sebastian, Hermes, Maven.

---

## 1. The Offer (locked)

**Founding Family membership = the HTA app + the founding box,
experienced as one product with a pre-season.**

- Join anytime Aug -> Sept 15. Enrollment CLOSES Sept 15 because
  the box order goes to production Sept 16. Deadline scarcity only -
  no numeric cap, no "spots left" language.
- On joining: install the PWA, welcome video from Coach Corinne,
  no-equipment family kickoff mission - founders are active from
  day one.
- Pre-season (Sept): thin, deliberate. One no-equipment mission
  every ~2 weeks + box-production updates ("your jerseys went to
  print this week" - made-to-order makes these genuinely good).
  Frame: every sports family understands pre-season.
- Season opener (Oct): boxes arrive, weekly missions proper begin,
  the app they already use lights up.
- Founder terms: founder pricing locked for life + first boxes
  ever made. (Optional flourish: founding family names in the
  program.)

## 2. Targets and the Floor Rule (locked)

- Floor 100 / goal 250 / stretch 500.
- Audience math: 2,000+ region families reachable as a
  complementary product to player development, plus camp families
  at peak warmth, plus referral compounding. 250 = 12.5% of
  reachable - ambitious, achievable with a real brand launch.
- **Floor rule, pre-committed: we ship in October at WHATEVER the
  Sept 15 number is, and iterate.** A low number is a marketing
  grade, not a launch blocker. No refund-and-retreat scenario.
  (Caveat handled in quoting: confirm per-box economics stay above
  water at the 100 tier.)

## 3. August = Brand Launch Campaign (Maven executes, Corinne
approves, StoryBrand framework)

- SB7 for HTA: hero = the sports parent who wants family life to
  feed the player, not compete with it; guide = Coach Corinne;
  plan = join founders -> pre-season -> box arrives -> weekly
  missions; stakes = another season of practices as drop-off
  logistics vs. a season the whole family plays.
- Channels, in trust order:
  1. Camp families - founders-first offer in final camp week
     (peak love, in-person mention + SMS, manifested).
  2. Referral mechanic BUILT INTO the founding offer - founders
     who bring a family get something real. Treat this as the
     product's most important feature through Sept 15; it is the
     only compounding channel.
  3. Kit lists (authenticated domain, nurture sequence).
  4. Social (accounts warming now) - shareable family-soccer
     content, not announcements.
  5. Region-adjacent reach: personal/business channels to the
     2,000+ families as a complementary product. Keep OFFICIAL
     region comms clean - personal networks and parent groups
     yes, region letterhead no. Protect the AYSO relationships.
- Campaign calendar: Maven drafts by July 20 for Corinne approval;
  content production late July; push begins Aug 1; crescendo
  Sept 8-15 (deadline week).

## 5. Build Sequencing (Hermes - unchanged from approved reorder)

1b -> recovery merge -> H1 (installable + trust/legal, Aug 1-14)
-> H2 (auth + payment + real backend by adapter + World Cup port,
Aug 15-31) -> then MC-1/2/3 (~Oct). Pre-season model adds no build
scope: it is content sequencing on the existing mission shape.

## 6. Go/No-Go (Sept 4 - all true or launch slips one week)

1. Real payment end-to-end, automatic access.
2. Magic-link auth on iOS Safari + Android Chrome.
3. PWA installs clean on both platforms.
4. Week-0 kickoff + welcome video + first two pre-season missions
   loaded.
5. Legal live: privacy, terms, refund, kids-data posture (parent
   owns account, kid profiles are parent-managed).
6. Confirmations firing (Kit email + SMS), sends manifested.
7. HTA backend fully separate from Mission Control's Convex.
8. Smoke test: 3-5 friendly families installed, paid (comped),
   completed kickoff without support.
9. Tiered quotes in hand, ship week stated in all offer copy,
   packing plan matched to realistic order range.

## 7. Not in v1 (park list)

Per-mission video (v1.1), reward shop redemption, help-button SMS
wiring, fantasy/bracket features, any speculative inventory beyond
the founder order, MC integration beyond internal dashboards.

## 8. Standing Risks

1. Corinne's August is the critical path: two curriculum blocks +
   box quoting while two AYSO seasons start. If anything slips,
   slip pre-season mission count, never weeks 1-4 content.
2. Referral mechanic underbuilt = 100 instead of 250. It gets
   designed like a feature, not a footnote.
3. Recovery merge dragging compresses H1/H2. Weekly Doctor tracks
   sprint aging.
4. MC ritual staging deferred to ~Oct - accepted, revisit if HTA
   slips past Sept 21.
