World Cup Family Prediction Challenge Rules

These rules are written to keep the challenge simple, family-friendly, and transparent. The challenge is free to enter and designed to help families turn the World Cup into easy soccer fun at home.

1. Sponsor

The sponsor and administrator is Home Team Academy, LLC. For questions, families may contact CoachCorinne@HomeTeamAcademy.com or +1 (805) 222-7442.

2. Eligibility

  • The challenge is open to families in the United States where permitted by law.
  • A parent or legal guardian who is at least 18 years old must submit and manage each family entry.
  • Children may participate through a parent-managed family entry. Kids should use first names, nicknames, or display names only.
  • Employees, contractors, and immediate family members of the sponsor may participate for fun but may be excluded from prizes.

3. How To Enter

  1. Go to the World Cup Family Prediction Challenge page.
  2. Enter a parent name, mobile number, family/team name, and participant display names.
  3. Choose the Quick Pick Bracket, Full Family Bracket, or optional Fantasy 5 path based on the participant age and preference.
  4. Submit the bracket before the applicable deadline.

The challenge is completely free to enter. There is currently no HTA product purchase, paid membership, or paid entry connected to this challenge.

4. Entry Periods

Full tournament bracket prizes Full Family Bracket entries must be submitted before the United States opener on June 12, 2026 at 6:00 PM Pacific / 9:00 PM Eastern. Late entries may still be accepted for activities or non-bracket prizes.
Quick Pick and family mission prizes Quick Pick prediction entries may stay open until every team has played its first group match, before teams begin their second group matches. Family missions may remain open during the tournament so younger kids and late-joining families can still participate.
Second chance entries Home Team Academy may open a second chance challenge after the group stage with separate scoring and smaller prizes.

5. Bracket Paths

Quick Pick Bracket

Recommended for younger kids. Participants choose one tournament winner and three surprise teams, then receive a kid-friendly printable activity sheet with flag coloring and simple country activities.

Full Family Bracket

Recommended for older kids, teens, and adults. Participants predict group winners, wildcards, knockout round winners, and the tournament champion.

Fantasy 5

Optional family-friendly player-pick path for anyone who wants to follow individual players without a complicated fantasy game. Participants pick 5 players, choose one captain, stay under a 30-point budget, and use no more than 2 players from one country.

6. Prize Categories

  • Overall Prediction Champion: family tickets to a local MLS or NWSL match, coordinated with the winning family based on location, family size, availability, and schedule, plus HTA credit and a mailed soccer surprise pack.
  • Second Place: $100 gift card to a family-friendly retailer such as Amazon, Target, Dick's Sporting Goods, or a soccer shop, plus a soccer surprise pack.
  • Third Place: $50 gift card plus a soccer surprise pack.
  • Second Chance Champion: separate post-group-stage prize, such as a $100 gift card or local soccer experience.
  • Fantasy 5 Champion: $75 soccer shop gift card or jersey credit, plus a mailed player-fan pack with soccer cards/stickers and HTA surprise items.
  • Best Captain Pick: $25 soccer shop gift card or soccer-card pack for the strongest captain selection.
  • Goal Getter Award: mini soccer ball or soccer-card pack for the best forward/goal-scorer pick.
  • Clean Sheet Hero Award: goalie gloves, mini ball, or soccer-card pack for the best Defender/Keeper pick.
  • Wildcard Hero Award: surprise soccer pack for the best lower-cost or unexpected Fantasy 5 pick.
  • Private Group Fantasy 5 Winner: small group bragging-rights prize or soccer surprise, separate from the main public leaderboard when available.
  • Kid Pick Award and weekly family mission awards: $10-$25 gift cards, mini soccer balls, soccer cards, Panini-style stickers, signed/autographed items when available, or other soccer surprises.
  • Participation Raffle: every eligible family that submits at least one bracket is automatically entered. Completing Fantasy 5 may earn 1 additional raffle entry per participant, capped at 3 Fantasy 5 bonus entries per family. Each eligible weekly family mission submission may earn one additional raffle entry, so families can keep playing even if their bracket gets busted.
  • Private Group Bonus: families may earn 1 additional raffle entry for each private group they join or create, up to 3 group participation entries total. Winning an eligible private group leaderboard may earn 3 additional raffle entries.

Prize details may vary based on winner age, location, family size, available donations, ticket availability, and fulfillment constraints. Prizes are non-transferable and may be substituted with a prize of similar value if needed. The challenge is free to enter.

7. Scoring

Main Bracket scoring:

  • Correct group winner: 3 points
  • Correct group runner-up: 2 points
  • Correct team advancing from group: 1 point
  • Correct Round of 32 winner: 4 points
  • Correct Round of 16 winner: 6 points
  • Correct quarterfinal winner: 8 points
  • Correct semifinal winner: 10 points
  • Correct champion: 20 points

Quick Pick scoring may include 20 points for the correct champion, 12 points for a correct finalist, 6 points for each correct semifinalist, and bonus recognition for strong surprise-team picks. Second Chance scoring starts fresh after group play and uses its own separate leaderboard.

Fantasy 5: families may build a simple 5-player squad with a 30-point budget, one captain, and no more than 2 players from the same country. Fantasy 5 points may be based on a player's country earning group-stage points for wins or ties, advancing, knockout wins, winning the tournament, player goals, player assists, defender/keeper clean sheets, captain bonus points, and wildcard hero bonuses.

Ties may be broken by correct champion, correct finalist, most correct knockout picks, most correct advancing teams, most family missions completed, or a random drawing among tied families.

8. Winner Selection and Notification

Winners will be determined from eligible entries using the published scoring system or activity challenge criteria. Potential winners will be contacted using the parent mobile number provided at entry. If a potential winner cannot be reached or does not respond within a reasonable time, another winner may be selected.

9. Family Missions

During the tournament, Home Team Academy may share simple family missions inspired by teams, countries, and soccer cultures. Family mission winners may be selected based on completion, creativity, family participation, or a random drawing from eligible submitted missions, depending on the specific mission instructions.

Family missions may include kid drawings, family team chants or songs, goal celebrations, country snack/watch party ideas, backyard skill missions, dream jersey designs, posters, or Coach Corinne's favorite family moment. Parent or guardian submission is required for any uploaded photo, drawing, audio, or video. HTA will ask before reposting child photo/video content publicly.

10. Privacy and Kids

  • Children do not need their own account.
  • Parents manage each entry and communication preference.
  • Kids should use first names, nicknames, or display names only.
  • Parent contact information is used for challenge administration, prize communication, bracket reminders, leaderboard/group updates, and family mission prompts.

11. SMS Updates

A parent mobile number is required to save and administer the challenge. Texts may include bracket confirmations, deadline reminders, group and leaderboard updates, family mission prompts, and prize notices. After the tournament, HTA may send one participation follow-up with a link for families who want more soccer-at-home activities. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out.

12. General Conditions

Home Team Academy may disqualify entries that are incomplete, fraudulent, abusive, automated, or submitted in violation of these rules. The sponsor may update, suspend, or cancel the challenge if technical problems, tournament schedule changes, fraud, or other events make the challenge unfair or impractical to operate as planned. Void where prohibited.

Source for tournament timing: FIFA lists the FIFA World Cup 2026 schedule from June 11, 2026 through the final on July 19, 2026. See FIFA's official schedule page at fifa.com.
